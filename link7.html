Palindrome

“Sometimes things are just same whether you start it from the beginning or from the end”. But we didn’t believe it is? So, to make sure whether it is the same or not we had made a program that checks whether the word or number you entered is a palindrome or not. Basically, palindrome means; a word, number, phrase which reads the same backwards as well as forward, such as madam, racecar etc… So, let’s have a look on it!
